About This App

What does this app do?

It is a simple web application which allows you to share your WiFi credentials instantly by scanning a QR code with a your smart device.

How does this work?

It generates a PDF file of a QR code, which contains your WiFi credentials. You can print out the PDF or save it to your computer / phone. Everyone can then simply scan the QR code to instantly connect to your WiFi.

Does this app store everyone's credentials in a database somewhere?

No, it does NOT store your WiFi credentials or any other kind of information about you.
